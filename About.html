About Us

Welcome to New Maa Kalyani Enterprises

MAYURI

With a focus on fostering a cleaner and more sustainable future, Mayuri E3W emerges as a flagship product under the umbrella of Saera Electric Auto Pvt. Ltd., a proud member of the esteemed Satra Group of Companies.


Drawing upon a decade-long expertise in crafting electric vehicles, Saera Electric Auto Pvt. Ltd. stands as a pioneering force in the industry. Our commitment extends beyond innovation; we strive to contribute meaningfully to society by leading the charge in manufacturing eco-conscious and emission-free vehicles.


At our core lies a profound belief in harnessing environmentally friendly technology. Our mission is clear: to offer a range of intelligent, chic, and comfortable vehicles that not only simplify daily commutes but also uphold our responsibility to preserve our planet.


We firmly believe that progress should not come at the cost of our environment. Through our endeavors, we aim to redefine mobility by prioritizing sustainability, paving the way towards a brighter and cleaner tomorrow.

Welcome to Mayuri, where sustainable transportation meets innovation. As a pioneering e-rickshaw company, we're dedicated to revolutionizing urban mobility while championing environmental consciousness. With a commitment to reducing carbon footprints and enhancing last-mile connectivity, Mayuri is poised to reshape the future of transportation.

At Mayuri, we believe in the power of electric vehicles to drive change. Our fleet of e-rickshaws embodies cutting-edge technology, offering efficient, reliable, and eco-friendly transportation solutions. Whether navigating bustling city streets or traversing narrow alleyways, our e-rickshaws deliver unmatched performance, ensuring a smooth and comfortable ride for passengers and drivers alike.

As a customer-centric company, Mayuri places paramount importance on safety, reliability, and affordability. Each e-rickshaw in our fleet undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure adherence to the highest standards of safety and durability. Moreover, our transparent pricing model ensures that our services remain accessible to all, empowering communities and fostering economic growth.
